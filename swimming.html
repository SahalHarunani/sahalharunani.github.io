Swimming Journey

From Fear to Excellence - A Remarkable Story

My Swimming Journey

2012

Age 4: The Beginning

Started at Bluefins Swim Club in Dar-es-Salaam. I was terrified of water initially, but with my parents' support, I joined the Nemo group and began my transformation.

2013

Age 5: Growing Confidence

Promoted to Ducklings group. Became more confident and eager for training. Participated in my first inter-school competition at Al Muntazir.

2014

Age 6: Competitive Start

Promoted to Starfish group. Started competing in national competitions as the youngest from my club. Won my first medals this year!

2016

Age 8: Elite Group

Promoted to the top Dolphins group. Won my first trophy. Medal count increased significantly. Started getting recognized by Tanzania Swimming Association.

2019

Age 11: National Team Selection

Selected for Tanzanian National Swimming Team! Represented Tanzania in CANA Zone 3 African Championships in Nairobi, Kenya.

Swimming Philosophy

"I am a person who never accepts defeat in anything regardless of what I do. I always like to win and be the top in everything. Whenever I go for trainings, I consistently go with a fresh mind, ready to achieve my goals. I always try to be focused, put in my maximum effort, and make sure that I feel the pain, cause that is the way I will improve in my swimming."
